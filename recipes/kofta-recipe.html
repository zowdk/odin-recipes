Ingredients

2P 4P
Tex Mex 5g 10g
Ground beef 250g 500g
Egg 1 2
Panko 30g 50g
Salt 1/4 tsp 1/2 tsp
Garlic cloves 1 2
Carrots 1 2
Lemon 1 1
Water 2,5 dl 5 dl/td>
Vegetable Bouillion 4g 8g
Couscous 100g 200g
Tomato 1 2
Cucumber 1 1
Spring Onion 1 bunch 1 bunch
Parsley 1 bunch 1 bunch
Yogurt 75g 150g

1. Season Meat

  • Blend Texmex, ground beef, egg, panko, and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
  • Form the meat into (6) x 2,5cm koftas (approx. 3 per person)

2. Fry the kofta

  • Heat a splash of olive oil in a pan on medium heat.
  • Fry the koftas for 10 min.
  • Rotate the kofta to cook evenly on all sides.
  • TIP: Koftas are done when the meat is cooked through with no sign of red.

3. Prep vegetables

  • Press or finely chop the garlic.
  • Shred carrots coarsely.
  • Slice lemon into wedges.
  • Dice tomato and cucumber coarsely.
  • Chop spring onion and parsleyfinely.

4. Cook couscous

  • Warm a splash of olive oil in a pot on medium-high heat.
  • Stirfry carrots and garlic for 2-3 min.
  • Add water and vegetable bouillon. Bring to a boil.
  • Add couscous and a touch of olive oil
  • Remove from heat, cover and let it sit for 5 min until water is absorbed.

5. Blend lemon yogurt dressing

  • Mix tomato, cucumber, some lemon juice, and half the spring onions and parsley with the finished couscous.
  • Add salt & pepper to taste.
  • Blend yogurt with juice from half the lemon and the rest of the parsley in a small bowl.
  • Add salt & pepper to taste.

6. Serve & Enjoy

  • Create a bed of couscous on a plate.
  • Top with koftas and remaining spring onion.
  • Garnish with lemonwedges and lemon dressing.
