Ingredients

2P 4P
Garlic cloves 2 3
Baguette 1 2
Chickpeas 1 can 1 can
Egg 2 4
Cucumber 1 2
Cherry tomatoes 250g 375g
Romaine lettuce 1 head 2 heads
Mustard 2 tsp 4 tsp
Mayonaise 50g 100g
Water 1 tsp 2 tsp
Shredded Parmesean 20g 40g

Prep raw ingredients

  • Heat oven to 200â„ƒ.
  • Boil a pot of water.
  • Press or finely chop garlic.
  • Crumble bread in smaller pieces.
  • Rinse chickpeas under cold water with a strainer.

Bake bread & chickpeas

  • Sprinkle bread and chickpeas on a baking sheet lined with baking paper.
  • Add garlic, olive oil, salt & pepper.
  • Bake for 8-10 min, or until crispy.

Boil eggs

  • Carefully set eggs in boiling water using a spoon.
  • Cook for 7-9 min.
  • Transfer to a bowl with cold water to cool.
  • Peel and halve.

Prep vegetables

  • Chop cucumber coarsely.
  • Halve cherry tomatoes.
  • Chop romaine lettuce.

Mix salad

  • Blend mustard, mayonaise, water, half of cheese, olive oil, salt & pepper in a bowl.
  • Add lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes and mix.

Serve & Enjoy

  • Serve in a deep bowl.
  • Top with croutons, chickpeas and egg.
  • Garnish with remaining cheese.
